Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "mod12counter.vhd"

The following waveforms show the behavior of lpm_counter megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "mod12counter.vhd". The design "mod12counter.vhd" is a 4 bit up modulus 12 counter. The counter has a synchronous clear input.

Fig. 1 : Wave showing counter operation.

The output port cout will be asserted at the completion of count sequence. The ports cin and cout are used to chain multiple counters together.
